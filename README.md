**CompanyCam** is a comprehensive tool designed to make photo and video documentation simple for contractors and project managers in construction. With **CompanyCam**, you can take detailed photos, annotate them with key information, and share real-time updates, all from the convenience of your mobile device. It helps keep everyone in the loop, enhancing transparency and teamwork throughout the project.
